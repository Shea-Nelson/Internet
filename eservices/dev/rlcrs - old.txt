			<h3 id="remit">Electronic File and Pay</h3>
			<div>
				<ul>
					<li><p>Records: Once a filing is submitted, it is stored for review in the Remittance History area until the detail 
					is posted and the payment is fully processed between financial institutions.</p></li>
					
					<li><p>Correcting a previous submission: As soon as you determine that a submission has been made in error (i.e., funds entered on incorrect line, incorrect 
					amount of funds entered, etc.), email the Department's <a title="Link to Revenue Accounting Email" 
					href="mailto:RevenueAccounting@floridarevenue.com">Revenue Accounting</a> section, or call 850-617-8586, to facilitate the correction.</p></li>
					
					<li><p>Replacing a failed electronic payment: Log in to the <a title="Link to Red Light Camera Remittance System - This link will open in a new window" 
					href="https://suntax.floridarevenue.com/" target="_blank">Red Light Camera Remittance System</a>, then select the "Returned Item" tab. Select the item that failed, 
					then click "Select" to proceed to the payment screen. Select the bank account from which to make the replacement payment, then submit payment. 
					<strong>Note:</strong> You do not need to re-enter line items or the dollar amount.</p></li> 
				</ul>
			</div>

			<h3 id="update">Updating Information</h3>
			<div>
				<ul>
					<li><p>WHERE DO YOU SEND THE REQUEST? Change local government name: Should you need to change the name of the local government, please send a request to the e-Services Unit explaining the 
					reason for the change and include official documentation to substantiate the change.</p></li>
					<li><p>Change your password: Log in to the <a title="Link to Red Light Camera Remittance System - This link will open in a new window" 
					href="https://suntax.floridarevenue.com/" target="_blank">Red Light Camera Remittance System</a>, then select the "Account Information" tab. Select "Change 
					User Password", enter the required information, then click "Save" for the change to take affect.</p></li>
					<li><p>WHERE DO YOU DO THIS? Contact Information on File and Pay page: Fill in the name, phone number and e-mail address of the person responsible for submitting 
					the revenue remittance. This will be the person the Department contacts if problems occur with the transmission/payment.</p></li>
				</ul>
